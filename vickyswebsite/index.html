About Me

Hello! I’m Thuy, but you can also call me Vicky. Growing up, i would always find myself immersed in the arts. From playing musical instruments, singing, drawing, and painting - it was my escape from the day to day. However, I had the opportunity to pursue a career in healthcare, thus keeping my desire for art as a hobby. During the pandemic, I began to return to art with a more tactile form in flowers. From making home arrangements to small gifts for friends, it renewed my love for creating art. Through much love and encouragement from friends, i decided to show this special world of mine to the public & launch in 2023. Thank you for visiting my page & joining me on this journey!
